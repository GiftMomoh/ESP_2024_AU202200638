Success Stories

Story 1

This is the story of how Ruby, a German Shephard found her forever home.
We found Ruby by the roadside one rainy day, frail and shivering from the cold and we took her in.She was with us for quite a while as she was a very mischeivious dog.A lot of families adopted her but brought her back as they found her too much to handle, some even said "Get rid of this wild beast,noone will ever adopt her" but we kept her and raised her until a navy seal who was going away for a while adopted aand trained her for his wife and children.
Now, Ruby still lives with the family and is quite protective of them especially the children.

Story 2

This is the story of how a rabbit name Fish was adopted.
Fish was adopted just a week after he was brought in.A young couple and their 8 years old daughter walked into the agency and when we took them to see the animals, their daughter was immediately drawn to Fish and begged her parents to adopt him and they did.
To this day,the young girl still sends me pictures of him.
